Youth Homelessness

The troubles of homeless youth remains largely misunderstood. Homelessness doesn't just affect people living on the streets, it includes people of all backgrounds who have no place to call home. Homeless youth includes people may be couch-surfing with friends, staying in shelters, in squats or on the street in alleys, doorways or parks.

Canadian Stats

  • 40,000 young people who spend some time homeless

  • 7,000 homeless youth on any given night

  • 20% of homeless population between ages 13 and 24

  • 40% of homeless youth younger than 16 when first experienced homelessness

  • 86% of homeless youth had been homeless before

  • 50% reported five or more episodes of homelessness

Who are Homeless Youth?

  • 80% reported leaving home because of family conflict

  • 63% reported childhood trauma and abuse

  • 60% reported some involvement with child welfare in the past

  • 83% experienced bullying in school

  • 50% reported being tested for a learning disability

  • 53% drop-out rate
